Smoked Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a smoked mac and cheese that is made using a Green Mountain Smoker

  • Macaroni
  • 1 brick of cream cheese
  • 1 cup each of Cheddar, pepper jack, colby jack
  • Cajun Seasoning *optional*
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1 can cream of mushroom
  • foil pan to put in smoker
  1. heat smoker to 250
  2. grate cheese
  3. cook macaroni until almost done
  4. put shredded cheese, cream cheese, and cream of mushroom in pan
  5. add some seaoning and then put on smoker for 20 min
  6. after 20 min pull out and add macaroni and sour cream-mix together well
  7. sprinkle leftover cheese and bread crumbs on top and add more Seasoning
  8. put back in smoker until melted cheese on top
