Breakfast Potatoes

Description

This is a breakfast potatoes recipe

  • potatoes any kind you like
  • seasonings of your choice
  • Ketchup
  • Tabasco
  • put potatoes in instant pot for 20 min, with a 10 min natural release
  • pull potatoes and slice into size of your liking
  • put potatoes in skillet with seasoning
  • cook until edges are crispy
  • eat with ketchup and Tabasco
